AR Museum Guidance

AR Guiding Application Design for Chimei Museum

To improve the experience of visiting Chimei museum, we propose an AR application to help guide visitors during the tour as well as entertain them with innovative AR functions.

I was a member of the team responsible for designing interaction models for the scenarios. This included guiding function, interactive games and archive function. We finally propose solid ideas to build the App.
