Love is in the Air

A user-centered design project focusing on couples in long distance relationships.

How I participated:

I was a member of the team responsible for designing interaction models for the emotional scenarios. This included editing emoticons, compass and scheduling messages. I finally created a prototype and conducted usability test.

This is a user-centered design project focusing on couples in long distance relationships. We investigated our target users, and finally proposed a novel application on a mouse with touch screen interface as the result. We developed several novel functions that help couple share emotion and targeted at the long annoying problem: time difference.

Timeline: 4 months

Design Problem

Couples tend to keep in touch and meetup regularly to maintain the relationship. Some of them might be disappointed by the distance or meetup frequency. We’d like to build a system to support these relationships, make them feel secure in the relationship.


Interview

What Did We Ask?

Before we start the interviews, we outlined the questions we were going to ask, and made sure that all the information we need will be provided.


Affinity Wall

We sort all the information from the interview into this affinity diagram.


Key Findings

- The contact timing, duration and frequency difference is the problem that most weekend couples encountered.
- A lot of them found that only when their life is not completely occupied by romance will they fit themselves in a state of comfort in their relationships.
- A date for weekend couples is precious, and that makes each part of the date become significant and meaningful.
- These couples tend to seek alternatives to maintain the romance, since they can’t see each other very often.

Why Remote Mouse

・Directly in touch with hand, a perfect medium to sense and deliver the warmth.
・Mouse is what all of our 3 personas use daily. Combined with the features we need, users don’t have to buy another product just for the romance. Also, it's easy to carry.
​ ・‘REMOTE’ mouse delivers the idea of long distance relationship. We want it to connect remotely not only to the computer but the partner.
