UX Workshop for STSP Startups

Workshop for Startups in Southern Taiwan Science Park to Embed Cutting-edge Techniques.

How I participated:

There are plenty of startups that have cutting-edge techniques, but have no idea how to put them into real use, or say more profitable use. In this workshop, we helped participants illustrate how their technology can participate, and how exactly the scenario would be like.

SCENARIO LAB was hired to organize the workshop to guide the startups illustrating the using scenario. I was a member of the team responsible for collecting all the materials before the workshop. This included coolhunting and card making. I guided the participant with ideation when they were in blank. I let them describe the scenario in very detail, and help them visualize it using handy tools.

Timeline: 2 months
