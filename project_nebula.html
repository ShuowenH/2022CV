How I participated:

The Nebula design system maintains visual voice of the brand is consistent throughout the entire experience. I was a member of the team responsible for designing the app ui. This included chapter illustrations to represent the header scene of each section and UI Glyphs for the app and nav bar.

To polish the user experience, we carried out JTBD research (helped us understand the users’ goals the app can accomplish), worked out all core User Flows, and came up with the Informations Architecture of the app.
